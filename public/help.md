## 💡 Help Center

### 🧠 What This Tool Does

This platform helps nonprofits write grant applications faster by reusing your existing content and generating tailored responses with AI—so you spend less time writing and more time on your mission.

### 🚀 How It Works

**1️⃣ Create a Grant Recipe**

Start by creating a Grant Recipe. This is where you give the AI context about:

- The grant opportunity

- The questions you need to answer

**2️⃣ Add Your Content**

Fill in or upload:

- A short description

- Supporting text (mission, programs, metrics, etc.)

- Supporting files (past grants, reports)

- Grant questions

The more context you provide, the better the results!

**3️⃣ Generate Your Grant ✨**

Click Generate and review your draft under Grant Proposals.
You can edit, regenerate, or reuse content anytime.

### ♻️ Reuse & Scale

Find all saved recipes under **Grant Recipes**

- Clone and regenerate for future grants

- Reduce repetitive work and stay consistent

Find all created proposals under **Grant Proposals**
