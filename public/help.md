## About
Pioneering personalized, evidence-based healing support for every survivor. Through research and innovative technology, Our Wave is developing proven pathways to healing that offer tailored support and promote long-term wellbeing. Their goal is to transform recovery, reducing negative health outcomes often faced by survivors. They combine science with compassionate care to empower survivors, fostering resilience and holistic healing.
## Problem
At Our Wave, the grantwriting process is burdensome and inefficient. Staff repeatedly repackage similar content such as mission, metrics, and narratives across grant applications, often manually copying and editing responses. This repetitive workflow not only wastes valuable time but also introduces inconsistencies, hinders scalability, and contributes to burnout among a small team already dedicated to supporting survivors of trauma.
## Solution
Digital Aid Seattle is partnering with Our Wave to develop a user-friendly grantwriting automation tool. This platform will centralize reusable content and leverage AI to generate tailored responses based on specific grant prompts. Features will include prompt guidance, tone customization, and seamless integration with Our Wave’s existing processes. This will ensure staff can produce high-quality applications with less effort while maintaining their authentic voice.
## Impact
The grantwriting tool will significantly reduce the time Our Wave staff spend on preparing applications, allowing them to reallocate focus toward their core mission of survivor support. By automating repetitive writing tasks and minimizing emotional labor, the platform will enhance funding capacity, reduce staff fatigue, and create a scalable, open-source resource for nonprofits beyond Our Wave.
